Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 8, 2025

At YouDown, we prioritize your privacy. As a non-intrusive utility service, we maintain a strict policy regarding the collection and use of user data.

Data Collection Policy (Zero Data Collection)

We are a zero-registration service. We do not require, collect, or store any personal data from our users, including names, email addresses, or demographic information.

Information Logged

Since video processing requires external network calls, certain non-personal data is briefly handled by our servers:

  • **Video URLs:** The link you input is processed momentarily to extract metadata and stream the video. It is not permanently logged or associated with any individual user activity.
  • **IP Address:** Your IP address is necessary for network communication and is logged by our hosting provider (Vercel/proxy service) for security and debugging, but we do not actively store or analyze this data for user identification.

Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies:

  • **Functional Cookies:** Necessary for site operation (e.g., tracking download state).
  • **Third-Party Advertising Cookies:** Google AdSense uses cookies to serve personalized ads. By using our site, you consent to the use of these cookies, which are managed directly by Google.

Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your non-existent identifiable information to outside parties. Video processing is managed by third-party libraries (like yt-dlp) and proxy services necessary for the tool's core function.
